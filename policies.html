2. Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At JENGAPAY, we value your privacy and recognize the importance of safeguarding your personal, business, and web data. This Privacy Policy details the exact parameters regarding how we collect, store, share, and protect your digital footprint.

JENGAPAY does not sell, lease, or monetize your private behavioral data to third-party advertising companies.
